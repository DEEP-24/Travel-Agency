Our Destinations

Charminar

Charminar is a historical monument located in the city of Hyderabad, India. It is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the city and is often considered to be the icon of Hyderabad. The monument was built in 1591 by the fifth ruler of the Qutb Shahi dynasty, Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah.

Paris

Paris is a city that is full of culture, history, and iconic landmarks. One of the most famous landmarks in Paris is the Eiffel Tower, but there are many other places to visit and things to see in this beautiful city.

Leaning Tower of Pisa

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Italy and the world. Located in the city of Pisa, the tower is known for its iconic lean, which is caused by the uneven settling of the ground on which it was built.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for its rich history and culture. From the majestic palaces and forts to the colorful festivals and traditional arts and crafts, Rajasthan is a living museum of Indian history and culture. The state is home to many UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the beautiful Hill Forts of Rajasthan.

New York

New York City is home to some of the most famous landmarks and attractions in the world, including the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Times Square, the Empire State Building, the Brooklyn Bridge, and the One World Trade Center.

San Diego

San Diego is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in California, including La Jolla Cove, Coronado Beach, and Mission Beach. Visitors can enjoy swimming, surfing, sunbathing, and beach volleyball at these stunning beaches.
